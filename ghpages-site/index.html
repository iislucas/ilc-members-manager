I Liq Chuan Portal

Internal tools, experiments, and documentation for the ILC Portal project.

Project Goals

The ILC Portal is designed to be a comprehensive platform for the I Liq Chuan community. Key objectives include:

  • Facilitating school management and providing students with information about I Liq Chuan. Including providing a form of digital passbook for members to track their progress and status.
  • Streamlining administrative tasks for Headquarters (HQ).
  • Maintaining a public directory of instructors and events.
